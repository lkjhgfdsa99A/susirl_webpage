Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can sign up using your Google account or email address. Simply tap "Join the fam!" on the welcome screen and follow the prompts.

What are vibes and how do they work?

Vibes are personalized daily moods and themes generated by our AI system based on your personality, location-specific trends, and what's popular in your area. They help guide your content creation and social interactions.

How do I add friends?

Use the search feature in the Friends tab to find users by username or email. Send friend requests and start connecting!

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information and posts.
